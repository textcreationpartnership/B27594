WHEREAS on Monday the 9th of this Instant January the Books for taking Subscriptions for Shares were
    _ An ANSWER to the Nameless Pamplet, assuming a Title of An Epistle to a Member of Parliament, concerning Mr. George Oldner's Invention; to prevent Ships from Foundering or Sinking at Sea, &c. it being presumed the Author was rather commanded by his Interest, than by a Member of the House of Commons.
